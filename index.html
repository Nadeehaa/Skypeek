About SkyPeek

Welcome to SkyPeek! 🚀

SkyPeek is a real-time weather forecasting app that helps you stay updated on current weather conditions and upcoming forecasts. Whether you need to check the temperature, humidity, wind speed, or severe weather alerts, SkyPeek provides accurate and reliable data powered by the OpenWeather API.

Features ✨

  • 🌍 Live Weather Updates – Get real-time weather data for any city.
  • 📅 7-Day Weather Forecast – Plan your week with daily weather predictions.
  • 📌 Saved Cities – Save your favorite locations for quick access.
  • ⚠️ Severe Weather Alerts – Stay informed about extreme weather conditions.
  • 🌡️ Temperature Units Toggle – Switch between Celsius and Fahrenheit.

Why Use SkyPeek? 🌈

  • 🌍 Global Coverage – Find weather details for any city in the world.
  • ⚡ Fast & Lightweight – Minimalistic design for a seamless experience.
  • 🎮 Retro Pixel-Art Theme – A fun, nostalgic look for weather updates.

Stay ahead of the weather with SkyPeek and make your day hassle-free! 🌤️
